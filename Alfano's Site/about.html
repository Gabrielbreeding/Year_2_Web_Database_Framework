What We're Doing to Protect Against The Virus

Here at Alfano's all employees are vaccinated, maintaining professional hygiene, and wearing masks
so that both you and them can feel safe even in this problematic pandemic.
We also now have agreements with doordash, grubhub, and other delivery services
to reduce contact and provide safer environments.
